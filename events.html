Introductory Session on Competitive Programming


On Wednesday, October 12, 2022 The CODEC club hosted it's second tutorial session of the academic year 2022-23 on the topic. “Introduction to Competitive programming” hosted by the club's Technical Lead Shubhaam Tiwary from 3rd year B. Tech CSIT. The event was successful in introducing and motivating the students about the exciting realm of Competitive Programming. The CODEC team further plans to continue on this lead and host a Coding Contest soon to give the students a real time experience of CP.



Academics Tutorial Sessions

On Tuesday, January 17, 2023 The CODEC club hosted it's third tutorial session of the academic year 2022-23 on the topic, “Programming in C & C++/ Android Application Development” hosted by the club's Management Head Pratham Dubey from III year B. Tech CSIT & Ateeb Ashfaque from IV year B. Tech CSIT respectively. The event was conducted for First year B. Tech CSIT to help them brush up their concepts on C & C++ programming and critical thinking skills. For the Second and Third year B. Tech CSIT students, the session was hosted to help them brush up their knowledge on Android development under the supervision of their seniors. The event was successful in providing the students with a great practice of their programming and development skill fundamentals along with motivating the students about the exciting realm of Android development and Programming.
